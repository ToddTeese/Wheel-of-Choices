Wheel of Choices!

Why Make This?

Making decisions from a group of options can sometimes be a pain. This is meant to make making decisions quick and fun.

Learning

That working with circles is a pain. But seriously, this project has been a good insight into a class based approach to breaking down objects. The wheel is constructed of wheel segments, by doing this it makes the addition/removal of wheel segments easier. It also helps to identify the segments by name and color, allowing for a smoother transition from user input to visual display.

I also need to add scaling for phones.
